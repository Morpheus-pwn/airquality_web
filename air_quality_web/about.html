About Us

We are a passionate team of engineers, and environmental enthusiasts dedicated to tackling air pollution through technology. Our project, the Real-Time Air Quality Monitoring System with IoT, is designed to provide accurate and real-time air quality data, empowering individuals and communities to make informed decisions about their environment.The implementation of this Air Quality Monitoring System not only enhances public awareness regarding air pollution but also facilitates data-driven decision-making for environmental policies

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop an accessible, scalable, and cost-effective solution for air quality monitoring. We aim to revolutionize air quality monitoring by enabling remote monitoring and analysis. The system is integrated with a web-based interface that allows users to visualize air quality metrics, receive real-time alerts for dangerous pollutant levels, and access historical trends.By leveraging IoT technology and the NodeMCU platform, we aim to enhance public awareness, promote environmental responsibility, and support data-driven policymaking.

Our Product Features

IoT-Enabled Wireless Connectivity

Uses Wi-Fi-enabled NodeMCU (ESP32) to transmit data to a cloud server, allowing remote access from anywhere.

Real-Time Monitoring

Monitors air quality parameters in real-time, providing instant updates on temperature, humidity, and air quality index.

Cost-Effective Solution

Offers a cost-effective alternative to traditional air quality monitoring methods, making it accessible to individuals and communities.

User-Friendly Interface

Provides a user-friendly interface, allowing users to visualize air quality metrics, receive alerts, and access historical data.

Easy Integration of Hardware

Integrates with common hardware components, such as temperature sensors, humidity sensors, and air quality sensors, making it easy to set up and maintain.

Scalability and Accessibility

Designed to be scalable and accessible to a wide range of users, including individuals, communities, and organizations.

Battery backup

Provides a battery backup to ensure uninterrupted operation in low-power environments.

Open-Source Software

Offers an open-source software platform, allowing users to customize and extend the system to meet their specific needs.
